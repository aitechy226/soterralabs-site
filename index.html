How we build

What you can count on.

Not slogans. Engineering commitments — learned from watching what breaks at scale.

01

You get a running system, not a deliverable.

Every engagement ends with something deployed in your environment — designed to your specs, running on your infrastructure.

02

Every decision is explainable.

Routing, scoring, and risk rules live in deterministic code — inspectable, testable, auditable. AI generates narratives and synthesizes results. It doesn't make decisions you can't trace.

03

Tested against what actually breaks it.

Happy-path tests prove nothing. Every system we deliver is verified against the failure it's supposed to handle — retries, timeouts, data edge cases, and the scenarios that only show up in production.

About

Soterra Labs.

Soterra Labs exists to put AI to work on real problems: faster decisions, better economics, and leaner operations.

We've been on both sides of the table — building systems as engineering leadership at Lehman Brothers and JPMorgan Chase, and selling them as Field CTOs at Dell. That dual perspective shapes everything we build: we know which questions buyers actually ask, and which answers hold up under scrutiny.

The technical foundation runs deep — real-time network configuration at Bell Labs, cloud stacks for FSI clients in regulated environments. Systems where failure had consequences.

Today our focus is the modern AI stack — foundation models, generative architecture, and production deployment on real hardware. Soterra Labs does not hand off to an outside engineering team; we are the engineering team.
