What we build

Two layers, one engineering practice.

Production AI systems

Our agentic systems are built on a single principle: deterministic code owns the decisions, LLMs own the prose. The LLM is used only for what it is reliable at today — narrative synthesis, classification within a constrained schema, summaries over structured input. ReAct loops and LLM-as-router patterns didn't meet the reliability bar for the systems we ship now; that may change as models do.

Pre-sale GTM intelligence

Our assessment tools turn vendor claims into procurement-defensible recommendations. GPU sizing grounded in math. Data-foundation audits that surface AI-readiness gaps before the pilot. Infrastructure validators that let hardware orders go in with confidence. Every recommendation, score, and threshold traces to a defensible source layer — first-principles math, vendor-published spec, or named engineering judgment.
