The Report

What the Prospect Walks Away With.

A personalized report — not a price list.

GPU Recommendation

A GPU recommendation matched to your workload by VRAM capacity, memory bandwidth, and compute throughput.

VRAM Fit Analysis

Whether your model fits on a single GPU or requires multi-GPU / multi-node. Precision-aware — INT8 at 30B gets a different answer than BF16 at 30B.

Cost Estimate

Monthly and annual cost estimate at the provider's actual rates, based on the GPU-hours your workload requires.

Compatibility Score

How well the provider fits your situation — workload match, infrastructure readiness, compliance coverage, and switching cost. With specific findings, not just a number.

Topology Detection

When VRAM exceeds single-GPU capacity, the report flags multi-node and specifies NVLink or InfiniBand requirements — automatically.

Switching Cost Assessment

How deep your current integration runs, classified as High, Medium, or Low with specific steps to evaluate.
