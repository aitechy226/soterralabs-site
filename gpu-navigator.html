Beyond the Report

What the Landing Page Didn't Tell You

The prospect sees a report. Here's what's running underneath — and why a form builder doesn't get you here.

Precision-Aware Sizing

INT8 at 30B gets a different GPU than BF16 at 30B. The recommendation adapts to quantization, not just parameter count.

Topology Detection

When VRAM exceeds single-GPU capacity, the report flags multi-node and specifies NVLink or InfiniBand — automatically.

Competitive Positioning

Your pricing vs competing options — at the prospect's exact GPU type and pricing model. You control which competitors appear and at what rates.

Switching Cost Assessment

Integration depth determines switching cost — classified as High, Medium, or Low with specific steps.

Blocker Detection

Named findings with specific penalties. A 55 with "contract lock-in (−25)" is actionable. A green checkbox isn't.

TCO as Internal Justification

3-year projection from actual GPU hours. This is the number the prospect's budget owner forwards to procurement.
